📘 Daily Vocabulary - Day 1

encounter
마주치다, 겪다
We encountered some problems on the way.
acknowledge
인정하다
She didn’t acknowledge my effort.
participate
참여하다
Are you going to participate in the event?
convince
설득하다
I finally convinced him to join us.
justify
정당화하다
How do you justify that decision?
interrupt
방해하다
Sorry to interrupt, but I have a question.
determine
결정하다
It’s hard to determine the cause.
contribute
기여하다
He contributed a lot to the project.
appreciate
감사하다, 진가를 알아보다
I really appreciate your help.
regret
후회하다
I regret not taking that opportunity.
maintain
유지하다
Try to maintain eye contact.
reflect
반영하다, 깊이 생각하다
That movie really made me reflect.
reassure
안심시키다
She reassured me that everything was fine.
emphasize
강조하다
He emphasized the need for honesty.
adapt
적응하다
It took me a while to adapt to this city.
hesitate
망설이다
Don’t hesitate to ask questions.
respond
응답하다
How did she respond to your message?
demonstrate
증명하다, 보여주다
He demonstrated how to use the app.
overcome
극복하다
He overcame his fear of public speaking.
assume
추정하다
I assumed you knew about it.
